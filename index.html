FAQ

What is Big Room?

Big Room is your community outreach, Facebook group and product management suite built into one. It is designed to streamline everything about your project and community.

When can I use Big Room?

We anticipate the first beta build will become available in the middle of November.

I already have a channel on IRC, will using Room disrupt our current community?

No. It is very important to us that existing communities will not be damaged by a migration. When starting your Room, make sure to click the "Add to external channel" button.

Want to build a better community?

100% no spam. You will be told when we launch, and maybe given a few promotion codes.
