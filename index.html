Opening Up Your Community

Room makes your community more open to newcomers

We are Open

When you use Room you are making a commitment to making community your open. We want to embrace this.

From our first release Room will be open too.

No Transition

If you already have an active community on IRC, that is totally fine! Room can run inside of your existing channel... Meaning that you don't have to migrate anyone if they don't want to!

FAQ

What is Room?

Room is a team based communication tool with a difference. You can think of it as a cross between Slack, IRC and your product growth and management suites.
We deeply encourage you to open your chat up to your community.

When can I use Room?

We anticipate the first beta build will become available in the middle November.

I already have a channel on IRC, will using Room disrupt our current community?

No. It is very important to us that existing communities will not be damaged by a migration. When starting your Room, make sure to click the "Add to external channel" button.

Want to build a better community?

We will remind you when we launch, and send you free invite codes to our early adopters.
